About the Festival

The Lanzones Festival is an annual celebration held every October in Camiguin to honor the province's sweetest export—the lanzones fruit.

It highlights the island’s rich agricultural heritage and pays tribute to local farmers who cultivate this prized tropical fruit.

Visitors can enjoy street dancing, colorful parades, cultural performances, and exhibits showcasing local products and traditions.
