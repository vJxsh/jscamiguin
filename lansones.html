About Lanzones

Lanzones is a small tropical fruit that has become Camiguin's most celebrated export. The island’s volcanic soil and tropical climate produce sweet, juicy, and flavorful fruits.

The fruit is harvested from August to October and plays a big role in the province’s culture and economy.

Camiguin celebrates the annual Lanzones Festival, featuring street dancing, parades, and cultural presentations in honor of the fruit and local farmers.
