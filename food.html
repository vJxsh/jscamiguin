Flavors of Camiguin

Savor the unique tastes and culinary treasures of the Island Born of Fire.

Vjandep Pastel

Camiguin's ultimate *pasalubong*. These are incredibly soft, sweet buns filled with rich, creamy yema (custard) that melts in your mouth.

Sweet Lanzones

Nourished by rich volcanic soil, Camiguin's lanzones are praised as the sweetest in the Philippines. Celebrated every October with a grand festival!

Kiping

A crisp, plate-sized snack made from sun-dried cassava paste. It is fried to a crunchy golden texture and generously drizzled with sweet, gooey coconut *latik*.

Chicken Surol

The ultimate Camiguin comfort dish. It features native chicken simmered in coconut milk, ginger, and *surol* leaves, yielding a rich, savory broth.
