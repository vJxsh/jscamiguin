Plan Your Adventure

Everything you need to know before visiting the Island of Camiguin

Best Time to Visit

March to May (Summer) is ideal for clear skies. However, visiting in **October** is perfect for the Lanzones Festival!

Getting Around

Rent a **motorbike** (₱350-500/day) for total freedom. For groups, hiring a **multicab** or a private van is a comfortable choice.

Safety Reminders

Camiguin is very safe. Always wear a helmet when riding. When snorkeling, avoid touching corals or sea life to protect the environment.

Cash is King

Most vendors and small restaurants only accept cash. ATMs are mostly located in Mambajao center, so withdraw early!

What to Pack

Bring quick-dry clothes, a waterproof bag for boat trips, and eco-friendly sunblock. Don't forget your reusable water bottle!
